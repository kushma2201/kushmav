Smart Health Consulting System

Consulting a doctor is a quite obvious thing in our day to day life, but the availability of the doctor during the time of our requirement is unpredictable. In order to overcome this issue a proposal of android application is made, this smart health application enables users to get instant report on their health issues through a intelligent health care application online.

OBJECTIVES

  • The “Smart health consulting” Android Application is helpful for patient to search the hospital based on specialist.
  • It simplify the task of patient and doctor.
  • It facilitates the interaction between patient and doctor.
  • It helps to optimize the work of patient and doctor.
  • Installation of the app in the Smartphone is quite simple and more useful to patients who have normal idea of android mobile.
